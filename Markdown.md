                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            ## Executive summary

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            This document is a BMAD (Business Model, Market, Advantage, Data) Phase 1 analysis draft for an unclaimed-money recovery service focused on the Houston metro (Harris County) with Texas as the initial operating jurisdiction. It compiles actionable findings, assumptions, recommended differentiators, a pricing proposal, data needs, and a phased 90-day roadmap. Where primary-source validation or competitor crawl is required, the document marks those items and lists clear next-step research prompts.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            HoustonClaim (placeholder name) helps Houston-area residents and small organizations recover legitimately-held unclaimed funds quickly and transparently. We combine a local outreach model (in-language community channels and partnerships with community organizations) with fast digital intake, secure ID verification, and a clear contingency or flat-fee pricing option — minimizing friction and maximizing claimant take-home while maintaining full audit trails for compliance.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            A) Flat-fee tier for claims under $500 (example $49–$99) — fast, predictable, good for high-volume small-claim segments.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            B) Contingency tier for larger claims — default 18% contingency with clear disclosures (alternative 12% for expedited service or 22% for complex/probate-handled claims).
