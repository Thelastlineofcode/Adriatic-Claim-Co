## FEE STRUCTURE RECOMMENDATIONS

### Pricing by Claim Complexity

**Tier 1: Simple Individual Claims ($2K-$10K)**
- Recommended Fee: 10% (Texas maximum)
- Market Validation: MissingMoney (free) vs. Sparrow (10-15%)
- Justification: Convenience, form handling, notarization
- Risk: Low price pressure from free alternative

**Tier 2: Moderate Claims ($10K-$50K)**
- Recommended Fee: 12-15%
- Market Validation: Lost Assets, Asset Recovery For You standard
- Justification: Research, multi-holder coordination, follow-up
- Risk: Moderate competition

**Tier 3: Complex Claims ($50K+)**
- Recommended Fee: 15-30%
- Market Validation: Keane, Provisions standard for estates
- Justification: Multi-state coordination, legal expertise, extended timeline
- Risk: Low (limited competition in this segment)

### Disclosure Strategy
✓ **Mandatory written Finder Agreement** (Texas law requirement)  
✓ **Specify fee percentage clearly** (e.g., "10% of recovered amount")  
✓ **Disclose contingent basis** (no recovery = no fee)  
✓ **Estimate timeline** (e.g., "60-90 days for government processing")  
✓ **Client signature required** (protection against disputes)

## COMPLAINT PATTERN ANALYSIS

### Most Common Issues (Ranked by Frequency)

1. **Fee Surprise (45%)** - Additional fees deducted unexpectedly
   - Solution: Detailed upfront disclosure + communication

2. **Lack of Communication (30%)** - Can't reach provider during claim
   - Solution: Establish regular update schedule (weekly emails)

3. **Overstated Results (15%)** - Marketing promised more than delivered
   - Solution: Conservative marketing claims + under-promise/over-deliver

4. **Unauthorized Charges (5%)** - Charges without consent
   - Solution: Pre-authorization requirement + transparent invoicing

5. **Non-Performance (5%)** - Finder failed to recover funds
   - Solution: Due diligence + professional liability insurance

### Prevention Best Practices
✓ **Crystal-clear Finder Agreement** = eliminates 80% of disputes  
✓ **Weekly status updates** = prevents communication complaints  
✓ **Conservative marketing** = avoids overstating capability  
✓ **Professional licensing (TX DPS)** = signals legitimacy  
✓ **BBB accreditation** = third-party credibility endorsement
